# How to identify fake text messages/SMS

Learn to identify fake text messages and safeguard your personal information.

Scammers may send text messages claiming to be Amazon. Be mindful if:

- You receive a text message sent from phone numbers that you don't recognize or from
  a number with a country code that differs from your country of residence.
- You receive text messages for orders or deliveries that you are not expecting.
- You receive text messages that contain phishing links with URLs that are misspelled,
  have typos, or have a link that is an IP address.
