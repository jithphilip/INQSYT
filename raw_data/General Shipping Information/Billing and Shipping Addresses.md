# Billing and Shipping Addresses

To avoid delays, provide error free billing and shipping addresses.

Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

- Your billing address must match the address on your monthly credit card or bank statement.
- Leave fields blank if they don't apply to your address, for example, don't enter
  "N/A," "Not applicable," and so on.
- Add information in the correct fields, for example, don't enter city and state information in the Address Line 2 field. Use the city and state fields instead.
- Avoid using accent marks or non-English characters. Our system can't process these
  characters.
- Don't enter shipping or delivery instructions in the address fields. Amazon, our merchants,
  and marketplace sellers use a variety of carriers. Therefore, we can't assist with
  requests for specific shippers.

## Add Business Hours to your Addresses

You can add hours of operation when you are shipping your orders to a business facility:

- Go to **Your Account**
- Select Your Addresses
- Go to the address you want to edit and select Add delivery instructions.
- Make sure your Property type is set to Business.
- Add the hours of operations for the business. You can set separate scheduling options
  for weekends, weekdays or holidays. You can also add information like access codes
  or additional delivery instructions.
