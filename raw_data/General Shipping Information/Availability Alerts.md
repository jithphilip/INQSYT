# Availability Alerts

If we don't have an official release date for an item, we may not be able to make
it available for pre-order. However, in many cases, you can sign up to be notified
when the item is available for purchase.

If we can offer this service for the item that interests you, an Alert Me box appears on the right side of the product information page. To use this service,
enter your email address in the space provided and select Sign up.

**Note:** Signing up for notification doesn't reserve the item for you. When you receive our
email saying that the item is available, return to our website to place your order
before the stock is gone again.

Prices on Amazon.com change frequently. The price in the email you receive is the best offer available
when the email was sent, but that price may have changed by the time you return to
the website to place an order.
