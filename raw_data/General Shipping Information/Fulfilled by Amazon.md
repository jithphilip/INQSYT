# Fulfilled by Amazon

Items "Fulfilled by Amazon" (FBA) are offered by a third-party seller, but packed
and shipped to you from an Amazon Fulfilment Center.

All Amazon standard shipping rates and policies apply to these items when shipped
domestically, including free shipping on qualifying orders and premium shipping.

When we ship your order, we indicate the carrier company, shipping date and estimated
delivery date on the **Your Account** page. You can track your package in Your Orders.

Sometimes we will send you the exact product you ordered from the inventory of another
seller when it is closer to you (with both sellers' consent, of course). When we do
this, we'll notify you in your shipping confirmation email and you can view information
about both sellers in your order Invoice, which you can find on Your Orders.

We also handle all customer service and product returns for "Fulfilled by Amazon"
items.
