# Secure Delivery with a One-Time Password

Due to the value of some items, a One-Time Password (OTP) is required on delivery
for some orders.

An OTP adds an extra layer of security to your packages.

If an OTP is required, we'll send a six-digit numeric OTP to your registered email
address, SMS or WhatsApp on the package delivery day.

You can also find the OTP:

- By selecting Track Package beside the order in Your Orders on the delivery day.
- By checking your registered email, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.

Your OTP is valid until the end of the delivery day.

Read the OTP that you’ve received to the driver in person and not over phone or intercom
to receive the package.

**Note:**

- If you're unable to receive the package, share the OTP  with someone you choose, and they can receive the package for you in person. We don't
  recommend sharing the OTP  with an unknown person.
- If no one can receive the package, we'll reattempt the delivery on the next working
  day.
- Don't add your phone number in the delivery address instructions.
- If you're not available to accept the delivery, ask the driver to reattempt the delivery
  when you or someone you choose will be at the delivery address.
- If you're not the package recipient or have ordered the item as a gift for someone
  else, forward the OTP to the recipient. Refer them to these guidelines.
