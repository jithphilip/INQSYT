# Return Fees, Damage Fees, and Partial Refunds

## Return Shipping Fees

Most items include at least one free return option.

Fees may apply when:
- Selecting paid drop-off or shipping methods
- Returning heavy or bulky items
- Having unusually high return rates

## Late Return Fees

Customers may incur fees when returns are not completed before the return deadline.

Fee structure:
- 20% of the item price during the first 30 days after the return-by date
- 100% of the item price afterward

## Damage Fees

Damage fees may apply if returned items:
- Are damaged
- Are missing parts
- Are not in original condition
- Have removed tags
- Show obvious signs of use

Fee structure:
- Up to 50% of the item price
- Amazon Luxury items may incur 100% damage fees

## Restocking Fees

A 100% restocking fee may apply to:
- Opened software
- Activated video games
- Opened collectible cards
- Board games
- Collectible figurines missing parts or packaging

## Partial Refund Policies

All returned items are inspected before refunds are issued.

Refunds may be reduced for:
- Customer use
- Damage
- Missing accessories
- Missing manuals
- Missing parts

## Currency-Converted Orders

For currency-converted orders:
- Refunds are issued in local currency
- Original exchange rates are used
