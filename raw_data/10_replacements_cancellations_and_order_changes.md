# Replacements, Cancellations, and Order Changes

## Replacing Damaged, Defective, or Broken Items

To replace an item:
1. Go to Your Orders
2. Select “Return or replace items”
3. Select a return reason
4. Follow on-screen instructions

## Replacement Orders

Amazon creates replacement orders using:
- The same shipping speed as the original order

Delivery dates may vary depending on:
- Inventory availability

## Return-By Requirements

To avoid charges:
- Original items must be returned before the Return By Date

In some situations:
- Amazon ships replacement items only after receiving the original return

## Out-of-Stock Replacements

If replacements are unavailable:
- Customers must return the item
- Place a new order separately

## Photo Verification Requests

Amazon may request photos clearly showing:
- Damage
- Defects
- Product condition

## Canceling Orders

Customers can cancel:
- Physical items
- Orders not yet in the shipping process

## Third-Party Seller Cancellation Rules

Orders sold and shipped by third-party sellers:
- Typically can be canceled within one business day

Once fulfillment begins:
- Seller approval may be required

## Canceling an Order

To cancel:
1. Go to Your Orders
2. Select View or edit order
3. Select Cancel items
4. Choose items to cancel
5. Submit cancellation request

## Orders That Cannot Be Canceled

If an order already shipped:
- Customers may refuse delivery
- Or return the package later

## Changing Order Information

Before dispatch, customers may update:
- Quantity
- Billing address
- Shipping address
- Payment method

## Mobile Device Limitation

Mobile devices may only support:
- Order cancellation

Additional editing options may require desktop access.

## Amazon Fresh Modifications

Customers can modify:
- Amazon Fresh deliveries
- Whole Foods deliveries
- Third-party merchant grocery deliveries

before fulfillment begins.
