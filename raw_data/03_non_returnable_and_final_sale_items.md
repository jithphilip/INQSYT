# Non-Returnable and Final Sale Items

## Non-Returnable Products

Some items cannot be returned or refunded.

These include:
- Perishable products
- Products posing health or safety risks
- Products with shipping restrictions
- Customized or personalized products
- Redeemable products
- Amazon Pharmacy products
- Pet medication products
- Certain digital products
- Automobiles

## Final Sale Products

Products labeled as “Final Sale” are:
- Non-returnable
- Non-refundable

Examples include:
- Trading card games
- Certain discounted items
- Amazon Haul items priced at $3 or less

## Damaged or Defective Non-Returnable Items

If a non-returnable or Final Sale item arrives:
- Damaged
- Defective
- Materially different from the order

Customers should contact Customer Service for assistance.
