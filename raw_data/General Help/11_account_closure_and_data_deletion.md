# Account Closure and Data Deletion

## Closing an Amazon Account

Customers can permanently close their Amazon account and request deletion of personal information.

## Steps to Close an Account

To submit an account closure request:
1. Go to Close Your Amazon Account
2. Sign into the account you want to close
3. Review products and services associated with the account
4. Select a reason for closure from the dropdown menu
5. Select the checkbox confirming permanent closure and data deletion
6. Select “Close my Account”

## Verification Requirements

Amazon sends a confirmation notification through:
- Email
- Text message

Customers must respond within 5 days to verify the closure request.

## Multiple Accounts

Customers with multiple Amazon accounts:
- Must submit separate closure requests for each account

This ensures Amazon has proper authorization for every account.

## Consequences of Closing an Account

After closure:
- The account becomes permanently inaccessible
- Closed accounts cannot be restored
- Customers must create a new account for future purchases

## Global Account Closure Effects

Closing an Amazon account affects:
- amazon.com
- amazon.fr
- amazon.com.mx
- Other Amazon global sites using the same credentials

## Impact on Other Amazon Services

After closure, customers lose access to:
- Seller accounts
- Brand Registry accounts
- Advertising accounts
- Other Amazon business services

## Reopening Access

Closed accounts cannot be reopened.

To use Amazon again:
- Customers must create a completely new account
