# Amazon Returns Policy Overview

## Easy Shopping and Simple Returns

Amazon is committed to making shopping seamless and worry-free. Customers may return eligible items when purchases do not meet expectations or needs change.

Most items sold on Amazon.com can be returned within 30 days of delivery for:
- Refund
- Replacement
- Exchange

Items must generally be:
- In original condition
- Unused
- Returned with original packaging and accessories

## Return Eligibility

Customers should review return eligibility:
- On the product detail page before purchase
- In Order History after placing an order

Eligible items may qualify for:
- Free returns
- Nearby drop-off locations
- Box-free returns
- Label-free returns

## Refund Eligibility

A refund is issued after:
1. Amazon or the third-party seller receives the returned item
2. The item is inspected
3. The return is approved

Return processing may take:
- Up to 30 days under normal conditions
- Longer in exceptional situations

If additional information is required, Amazon contacts the customer with further instructions.
