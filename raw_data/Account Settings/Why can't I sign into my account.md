# Why Can't I Sign into My Account?

If you can't log into Amazon, you may be entering incorrect account information or
you may need to reset your password.

If you can’t sign into Amazon, verify the following before resetting your password:

- **Email Address:** Your email address must be in the format username@domain.com (.net, .org). If you
  have multiple email addresses, make sure you're using the correct email address and
  password combination.
- **Mobile Phone Number**: If you created your account with a mobile phone number, you'll need to use this
  number to log in. Make sure to include your country code.
- **Password:** Check CAPS Lock and NUM Lock on your keyboard. Passwords are case-sensitive, so "PASSWORD" and "Password" are recognized as two different passwords.
- **One-Time Password (OTP) or Security code:** If you didn't receive the OTP via SMS, select "Resend OTP" on the verification page.
  You'll also see an option to "Send OTP by WhatsApp." This allows you to receive the
  OTP through a WhatsApp account linked to your registered phone number on Amazon.

If your email address and mobile phone number are correct, proceed to Reset Your Password.
