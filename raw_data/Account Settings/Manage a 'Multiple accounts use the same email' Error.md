# Manage a 'Multiple accounts use the same email' Error

If you get an error saying 'Multiple accounts use the same email address' when trying
to sign in to Amazon, follow these steps to troubleshoot.

Amazon no longer supports using the same email address to create or sign in to more
than one account. If you receive this error, you must either sign in to the default
account for your email, which is the one you signed into most recently, or change
the email for your secondary account.

You cannot use the same email address to create or sign in to more than one Amazon
account. To fix the issue, sign in to your main email account, the one you used last,
or update the email address for your secondary account.

**You received this error message and you don't recall having multiple Amazon accounts:**

This error is not an indication that your account security is compromised. It can
happen if:

1. You unintentionally made another Amazon account before, possibly when you got locked
   out and had to create another one.
2. Someone else may now own your old email address and used it to create an Amazon account.
   They don't have access to your Amazon account.

**You do not intend to keep using this secondary account:**

Select the
Sign in to default account button. Sign in using the password for your default account. If you've forgotten
your password, you can
reset your password. To delete your secondary account, first change the email address, and then sign
in. After you've signed in,
request closure of your Amazon account.

**To continue using this secondary account:**

Select the
Use this secondary account option. Follow the steps for changing your email address.

If you want to sign in to your secondary account without changing email, set it as
your default account. When prompted to change your email address, select
Or keep using [email] for this account.. You will be guided through the process of making your secondary account your default
account. Your previous default account will still exist. However, at sign in you will
be asked to change the email.
