# Change Your Subscription Email Preferences

You can use the links below to set your subscription email preferences.

To change your preferences for a specific subscription option:

1. Select the appropriate link from the following list:
   - Your Amazon Prime Membership
   - Your Subscribe & Save Items
   - Communication Preferences Center (for deals, product recommendations, new releases, and more.)
   - Amazon Delivers Email Subscriptions
2. Review your current preferences and make any desired changes.

   It may take 1-5 business days for your new preferences to take effect.
