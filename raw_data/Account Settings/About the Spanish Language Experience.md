# About the Spanish Language Experience

About the Spanish Language Experience on Amazon.com

As a Spanish speaking customer you can enjoy a more convenient shopping experience
on Amazon.com by switching your Language Settings from English to Spanish. You can shop from the vast selection on Amazon.com with
millions of translated product detail pages and many other helpful parts of the website
like the "Your Account" section in Spanish. The service provides navigation, customer service with email
and phone support, and order and shipment information in Spanish.

We're continuously improving the Spanish language experience on Amazon.com. While
many of our features are in Spanish, there are some site features or communications
which are not available in Spanish at this time. If you run into a problem you can't
solve yourself, our Spanish language Customer Service team can be contacted in the
Customer Service Center.
