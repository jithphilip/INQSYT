# Change Your Password

You can change your Amazon password by selecting Login & Security in Your Account.

To update your password, go to

Login & Security

To change your password:

1. In Your Account, select Login & Security.
2. Next to Password, select Edit.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions and select Save changes.

If you've forgotten your password, go to Reset Your Password.
