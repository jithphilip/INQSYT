# Manage an ‘E-mail address already in use’ Error

If you see an 'E-mail address already in use' error when trying to use an email address
on an Amazon account, follow these troubleshooting steps.

The same email address can't be used for multiple Amazon accounts. An ‘E-mail address
already in use’ error message will be displayed if you attempt to use the same email
address on more than one Amazon account.

**If the error occurs when trying to create a new Amazon account:**

1. Attempt to sign into the Amazon account with your password. If you have forgotten
   your password, you can reset your password.
2. If you're still unable to sign in and you're certain you've never created an Amazon
   account using this address, try creating an account using:
   - another email address you own.
   - an email alias. Check with your email provider if they support creating an email alias
     with your email account.
   - your mobile phone number.

**If the error occurs when trying to update the email address on your Amazon account:**

1. You may already own an existing account with that email address.
2. To use that email address on another account, you need to release it from your existing
   account. To release the email address:
   1. Sign in to your existing account that already uses the email address. If you don't
      remember the password, reset your password.
   2. Change the email address on your existing account to a different email address you
      own.
   3. Sign out of your existing account.
   4. Sign into the current account and update the email address, to the email address you
      released from the existing account.
