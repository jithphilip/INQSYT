# Change Your Account Settings

You can change your phone number, email address, name, password, or other Amazon account
information.

To manage your account settings, visit

Login and Security

How to Change Your Account Settings :

1. In Your Account, visit Login and Security.
2. Next to the account information
   you'd like to update, select Edit.
3. Follow the on-screen
   instructions and select Save
   Changes.
4. After you've completed all your
   updates, select Done.

Reset Your Password:

If you've forgotten your password, visit Reset Your
Password.
