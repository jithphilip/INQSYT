# Sign out of Your Amazon Account in the Amazon Shopping App

You can sign out of your Amazon account using the following steps.

To sign out of your Amazon account in the Amazon shopping app on Android and iOS mobile
devices:

1. Open the app and select the hamburger button on the bottom right corner of the screen.
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
3. Select Sign-out and confirm.
