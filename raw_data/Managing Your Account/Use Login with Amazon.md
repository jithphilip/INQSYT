# Use Login with Amazon

Login with Amazon allows you to easily and securely use your Amazon account to sign
into and share selected data, based on your consent, with third-party websites or
apps.

How to Use Login With Amazon

1. Go to a website or app that offers Login with Amazon.
2. Select Login with Amazon. 

   If our system doesn't detect an authenticated Amazon account, the Amazon-hosted sign-in
   screen will appear.
3. Sign in with your preferred method.
4. You'll be directed back to the website or app as a registered user, or prompted to
   complete registration.

Security:

Third parties are companies or developers who aren't Amazon. Only share your data
with third parties you trust.

When you use Login with Amazon, you'll always get an Amazon sign-in page with an Amazon
web address. You won't have to give your Amazon password directly to any other website
or app.

Managing Third-Party Access:

The first time you sign in, a screen requesting your consent to allow the third-party
application specific access to your data will appear. If you'd like to allow this
access, select Allow.

You can remove third-party access at any time by visiting Manage Login with Amazon.
