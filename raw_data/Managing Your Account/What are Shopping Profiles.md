# What are Shopping Profiles?

For a personalized experience in a shared account, create a shopping profile.

Shopping profiles give adults
and teens in certain jurisdictions
who share an
Amazon account a more personalized experience. Profiles use activities like searches and
browsing history to give tailored recommendations to the shopper. To add
a shopping profile
to your account, go to
Add a Shopping Profile

Using
adult
shopping profiles won't change how you shop on Amazon. Activities that take place
within the account will continue to be the responsibility of the account holder. For
more information, refer to our

**Note:**

- The nature of account sharing allows customers to have access to all the information
  under the account, such as wallet, address and order history. Setting up shopping
  profiles will not enable any additional privacy between customers who share accounts.

  Shopping profiles are intended for adult shoppers.
