# Use Switch Accounts

Switch Accounts allows you to add and switch between Amazon accounts without having
to sign out and in.

**Important:** To prevent others from accessing your Amazon accounts, we recommend that you don't
use this feature on shared devices and computers.

To switch or add accounts in a browser:

1. In the Account & Lists menu, select Switch Accounts.
2. To add a new Amazon account to the browser, select Add account. Enter your account credentials and follow the instructions on the screen.
3. To switch between accounts, select the account you want from the Switch Accounts page.
