# Manage third-party apps & services with data access through Login with Amazon

Login with Amazon allows you to easily and securely use your Amazon account to sign
in to and share selected data based on your consent with third-party websites or apps.
If you no longer want to allow third-party apps or services to access your data, you
can remove their connection to your Amazon account.

To remove third party access:

1. Make sure you're signed into the Amazon Account.
2. Go to Your Account > Manage your data > Manage apps & services with data access.
3. Select Remove next to the appor service whose connection you want to remove.

   **Note:** Even if you remove access, the service can still use the information you previously provided. If you no longer want to get
   emails from a website or app using Login with Amazon, contact the provider directly
   for their unsubscription procedure.
