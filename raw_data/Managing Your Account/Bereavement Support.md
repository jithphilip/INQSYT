# Bereavement support

We're sorry for your loss. Our dedicated support team is here to help.

## Protecting account information

To ensure privacy and security, we can only share account details or take most actions
related to an account when the request comes from the authorized estate manager.

If you can't provide all the required documents for authorization, we can still assist
with:

- Terminating subscriptions
- Stopping recurring deliveries
- Deactivating the account

For support regarding these topics, please email
bereavement-support-cs@amazon.com. Be sure to include the death certificate and the email or phone number associated
with the account.

## You have access to the email address or phone number associated with the account

If you have access to the email address or phone number associated with the account,
you can:

1. Sign in to the account to manage or close it.
2. Reset the password using the Forgot password link.

   Further guidance can be found on our
   Reset your password Help page.

## You don't have access to the account

To request account information, close an account, or make changes without having
access, verification is required.
This verification ensures that we only act on requests from an authorized representative.

To verify your authority, we require the following:

- **A copy of the death certificate,**
- **Proof of authorization** showing you're the legal representative of the deceased's estate (such as a probate
  or executor documentation),
- **A valid photographic ID** (such as a passport, driver's license, or ID card) of the authorized person, and
- **The email address/phone number** linked to the Amazon account.

If you don't know the email address, we need additional information to help us locate
the account. This additional information could be, for example, a unique charge ID
or order number. Those unique numbers can be found on delivery packages, confirmation
emails, or payment statements.

Once you've gathered the required documentation:

- Email it to
  bereavement-support-cs@amazon.com.
- Our team will verify the documents and act on your request.

All documents will be securely deleted after validation.
