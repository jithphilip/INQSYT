# Archived Orders

You can view previously archived orders in Your Orders.

To view previously archived orders:

1. Go to Your Orders.
2. - Search for the relevant order, or
   - filter for the date range that the order was originally purchased.

**Maintaining separate order histories**

For multiple family members to have separate order histories, consider using Amazon Family to link accounts. Linked accounts share select Prime benefits, and digital content
using Family Library.
