# Sign out of Your Amazon Account on the Amazon Website

You can sign out of your Amazon account by selecting Sign Out in the Account & Lists drop-down or selecting your name if your are using a mobile browser.

To sign out of your Amazon account on the Amazon website:

1. Select Account & Lists(desktop browser) or select your name (mobile browser).
2. In the menu that appears, select Sign Out.
