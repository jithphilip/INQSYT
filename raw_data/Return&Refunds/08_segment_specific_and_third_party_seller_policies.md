# Segment-Specific and Third-Party Seller Policies

## Amazon Pharmacy

Amazon Pharmacy purchases are:
- Non-returnable
- Non-refundable

Customers should consult Amazon Pharmacy Help for support.

## Amazon Business

Standard Amazon return policies also apply to Amazon Business purchases.

Special programs such as:
- Spot Buys
- Custom Quote purchases

may have separate terms and conditions.

Customers should consult:
- Amazon Business Accounts Terms and Conditions
- Feature Terms and Conditions

## Amazon Luxury

Amazon Luxury items may include:
- Specialized support procedures
- Different damage fee structures

Customers should use the Amazon Luxury Help Page for assistance.

## Third-Party Seller Returns

For orders fulfilled and shipped by third-party sellers:
- Returns are sent directly to sellers instead of Amazon

Third-party sellers must provide one of the following:
- A return address within the United States
- A prepaid return label
- A full refund without requiring the item to be returned

## A-to-z Guarantee

For issues involving:
- Third-party sellers
- Seller-shipped products
- Seller-related disputes

Customers may use Amazon’s A-to-z Guarantee protection.
