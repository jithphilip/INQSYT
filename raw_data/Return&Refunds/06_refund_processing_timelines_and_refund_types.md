# Refund Processing, Timelines, and Refund Types

## Refund Processing

Refunds are processed after Amazon receives and approves the returned item.

Customers receive email notifications with refund summaries after refunds are issued.

## Refund Timelines by Payment Method

| Refund Method | Estimated Timeline |
|---|---|
| Amazon Gift Card | 2–3 hours |
| Credit Card | 3–5 business days |
| Shop with Reward Points | Up to 5 business days |
| Debit Card | Up to 10 business days |
| Checking Account | Up to 10 business days |
| EBT Card | Up to 10 business days |
| Cash at Participating Locations | Up to 10 business days |
| Prepaid Credit Card | Up to 30 days |
| Promotional Certificate | No refund issued |

## Types of Refunds

### Advanced Refunds

Advanced refunds are issued when the carrier first scans the return instead of waiting for Amazon to process the item.

### Declined Refunds

If the original payment method cannot receive funds:
- Refunds are issued to Amazon account balance

Example:
- Expired credit cards

### Partial Refunds

Refund amounts may be reduced when items are:
- Used
- Damaged
- Missing parts

### Gift Refunds

Gift return refunds are issued to the person who initiated the return.
