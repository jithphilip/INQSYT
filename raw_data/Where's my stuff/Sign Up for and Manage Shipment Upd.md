# Sign Up for and Manage Shipment Updates via Text for All Orders



You can sign up to receive tracking information for your orders via text message when there are exceptions or delays



To sign up for or cancel shipment updates via text, or change your phone number:



1. Go to Your Account.
2. Select Shipment updates via text under the Communication and content section.



Note: Shipment updates via text are only available for packages shipped by Amazon.com sellers.
