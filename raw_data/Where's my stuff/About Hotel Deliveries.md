# About Hotel Deliveries



Shipping items to a hotel may result in complications not covered by Amazon.



If you choose to ship an item to a hotel, the following policy applies:



Provide complete address details, check delivery times and communicate with the hotel.



##### Before placing your order:



* Contact the hotel. Ask them about their policies for accepting packages. Confirm whether they need any specific information to ensure that you successfully get your item. Ask about any related fees and how early you can send the item.
* Add details to your address. You can add a new address to your Address Book. You can also add or modify instructions to existing addresses by selecting Add Delivery Instructions or Edit on the desired address. Include the following details:

  * Recipient’s full name (name on the reservation)
  * Name of the hotel
* Check your delivery times. Make sure that your delivery date during checkout matches when you are at the location.



##### After ordering:



* Inform the hotel. Let the front desk know that you’re expecting a package.
* Track your package. You can find tracking numbers and delivery updates in Your Orders. Visit the Tracking Your Package Help page for more details.



Amazon cannot issue refunds or replacements for packages that are delivered to a hotel but not received by the customer.



A delivery is considered successful once the package receives a delivery scan at the hotel’s front desk, concierge desk, or designated receiving area. This means Amazon will not provide a replacement or refund for any packages marked as delivered to these hotel locations but later reported missing by the guest.



For all other hotel deliveries where the package is successfully received by the customer, our standard return and refund policies still apply.



##### Find a Missing Package that Shows as Delivered



Follow the institution’s instructions for collecting packages in a timely manner. Look for any notifications sent to your phone or email.



If your package was scanned as Delivered but you didn’t receive a notification from your institution, check with the receiving department or receptionist. Have your name and tracking number ready before you speak with them.



Follow the steps in Find a Missing Package that Shows as Delivered.
