# Push Notifications for Shipment Tracking



You can set up or edit push notifications to track your orders.



To receive push notifications for shipment tracking, ensure your phone settings allow app notifications and then turn on push notifications in the Amazon app.



##### Enable Push Notifications on Your Device



For iOS:



* Ensure you have the Amazon app (Go to the App Store and search Amazon)
* Go to Settings > Notifications > Amazon > Allow Notifications
* Turn on “Time Sensitive Notifications” and select all alert options for best notification of delivery



For Android:



* Ensure you have the Amazon app (Go to the App Store and search Amazon)
* Go to Settings > Notifications > App Notifications > Amazon > Turn on
* Turn on “Time Sensitive Notifications” and select all alert options for best notification of delivery



##### Check Your Push Notifications in the Amazon App



Go to the Amazon app > Your Account (Person icon in the bottom left corner of the app) > Settings (cog icon in the upper right hand corner of the app) > Notifications > Account and shipping. Turn on all for Push notifications.
