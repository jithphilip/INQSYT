# About Currency of Preference on International Shopping

Currency of Preference on International Shopping allows you to shop in over 80 currencies.

Please note: Currency of Preference and currency settings are not available for domestic
US shoppers.

Amazon.com allows you to shop in over 80 currencies. Currency of Preference is a display-only
feature and allows you to see prices displayed in your preferred currency. You can
update your Currency of Preference by changing from USD (default currency) to your
preferred currency in the currency settings page. The available currencies for shopping
are listed below. Once you are ready to purchase your item, at checkout, an 'Order
Total' will display the total price of the order in the preferred currency. For payment,
a 'Payment Total' will display the amount your payment method will be charged in.

If your order is eligible at checkout for Amazon Currency Converter, you can elect
to pay with an eligible card, and the Payment Total will display the price in your
card currency you elected to pay in. Please note that payment in a different currency
is only possible for orders eligible for Amazon Currency Converter.

The currency you select is for reference use only. Prices may be displayed in your
selected currency for your convenience, and you can have prices displayed in your
chosen currency, even for orders not eligible for Amazon Currency Converter. At checkout,
if you wish to pay using your chosen currency, the actual sales price will have additional
fees which will be calculated at checkout. Your order must satisfy the following requirements
to be eligible for paying in your preferred currency with the Amazon Currency Converter.

**Note:**

- At any point within shopping, you can update your currency by changing to your preferred
  currency in the currency settings page. To use Amazon Currency Converter, proceed
  to checkout to determine eligibility. In order to see whether your order is eligible
  for checkout with the Amazon Currency Converter, select items you'd like to purchase
  and proceed to checkout.
- If the order is ineligible for checkout with the Amazon Currency Converter, your card
  will be charged in USD (default currency). Depending on your card's local currency,
  your bank or credit card company may assess additional fees, which may increase the
  overall cost of your purchase. Please contact your bank regarding these fees. Your
  local currency refers to the card currency associated with your eligible payment method.
- If you change an order, the Amazon Currency Converter will recalculate eligibility
  based on your changes. In the event that these changes make your order ineligible
  for the Amazon Currency Converter, your order total can only be paid in USD.
- You can learn more about exchange rates here.
